# Customer Persona Templates

Use these templates to build realistic customer personas for sales role-play.

## Persona Structure

Each persona should include:

```json
{
  "name": "Customer name",
  "role": "Job title / Role",
  "company": "Company name and description",
  "industry": "Industry sector",
  "company_size": "Number of employees",
  "pain_points": ["List of challenges they face"],
  "goals": ["What they want to achieve"],
  "objections": ["Common concerns they raise"],
  "buying_motivations": ["What drives their decisions"],
  "communication_style": "How they prefer to communicate",
  "decision_authority": "Can they decide alone or need approval",
  "budget_sensitivity": "High/Medium/Low"
}
```

## Example Personas

## Behavior Guidelines for Role-Play

When playing a customer persona:

1. **Stay in character** - Maintain the persona's communication style
2. **Raise objections naturally** - Don't make it too easy
3. **Respond to good questions** - Reward the salesperson's discovery
4. **Escalate or de-escalate** - Based on the salesperson's approach
5. **Have a breaking point** - Be willing to "buy" if truly persuaded
6. **Provide feedback cues** - Show engagement or disengagement

## Difficulty Levels

- **Easy**: Cooperative, open about pain points, ready to buy
- **Medium**: Some objections, needs convincing, has questions
- **Hard**: Skeptical, busy, multiple objections, hard to reach
- **Expert**: Hostile, time-limited, aggressive negotiator
