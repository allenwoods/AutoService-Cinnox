# Customer Persona Templates

Use these templates as starting points for creating realistic customer personas for training simulations.

---

## The Frustrated Tech User

```json
{
  "name": "Zhang Wei",
  "type": "returning",
  "background": "Software developer, has been using the product for 6 months, generally satisfied but frustrated by recent update",
  "issue_type": "Technical issue - software not working after update",
  "emotional_state": "frustrated",
  "communication_style": "technical",
  "expectations": "Quick resolution, technical explanation of what went wrong",
  "difficulty": "medium",
  "special_needs": "None"
}
```

**Behavior notes**: Will use technical jargon, expects competent technical support, may become impatient if support agent doesn't understand the issue.

## Creating Custom Personas

When creating your own personas, include:

1. **Realistic background**: Give context that explains their behavior
2. **Clear issue type**: Define what they're calling about
3. **Emotional state**: Start angry/frustrated for challenging scenarios
4. **Communication style**: Affects how they express themselves
5. **Expectations**: What they hope to achieve
6. **Difficulty level**: For training progression
7. **Special considerations**: Accessibility, language, time constraints

The best personas have internal consistency - their behavior should make sense given their background and situation.
